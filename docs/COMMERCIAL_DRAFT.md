# STG Engine — Commercial License

STG Engine is **free** for personal, academic, educational, non-profit,
government, freelancer, and open source use under the
[Business Source License 1.1](LICENSE).

For-profit company use requires a **separate commercial license**.

---

## Who needs a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if you are:

- A for-profit company (any size — startup, SMB, enterprise) using STG Engine
  in any business activity
- A SaaS or API provider where STG Engine is part of your offering
- An organization integrating STG Engine into a closed-source commercial product
- A reseller, OEM, or service provider redistributing STG Engine
- A business deploying STG Engine in production for internal operations

This applies regardless of company size.

## Who does NOT need a commercial license?

You can use STG Engine freely without any payment or registration if you are:

- An individual developer working on personal or hobby projects
- A student or academic researcher (any institution)
- An educational institution (school, university, online course)
- A non-profit organization
- A government agency or public research institution
- An open source project (any OSI-approved license)
- A self-employed developer / freelancer working on your own projects
  (client deliverables for for-profit companies require a commercial license)
- Anyone evaluating STG Engine before potential commercial deployment
  (30-day evaluation period; contact us before going to production)

## How to obtain a commercial license

Email **contact@stl-lang.org** with:

1. Your company name and website
2. A brief description of how you plan to use STG Engine
3. Approximate company size (employees, revenue tier, funding stage)
4. Required deployment scale (single instance, multi-tenant, etc.)
5. Timeline

We respond within **5 business days** with one of:

- A commercial license offer with pricing
- A request for clarification
- An explanation if your use case actually qualifies for the free license
- A free / discounted commercial license for early-stage startups,
  contributors, and aligned research projects

## Pricing

STG Engine is in alpha. Pricing is not yet established — contact us

## Why this model?

STG Engine represents years of original research grounded in Density Monism
and cognitive architecture theory. We are committed to keeping STG
**free** for individual developers, researchers, students, and the
open source community — these are exactly the people who advance the field
and benefit most from open access.

We ask companies that profit from STG Engine to contribute back financially.
This funding lets us:

- Continue active development (Rust core, new algorithms, integrations)
- Maintain pre-built wheels for all major platforms
- Respond to user issues and requests
- Keep STG Engine free for everyone else

If you believe in this model, please respect it. If your organization
benefits from STG, contact us.
